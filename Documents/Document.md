## Distortion Data
The measured total harmonic distortion on the receiving end of a Chatter Cuff to Chatter Cuff test is 6.85% or -23.29dB. This test was performed by transmitting a known signal (1kHz tone) from one Chatter Cuff to the other in direct line of sight.
